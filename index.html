About me

Who I Am

Hi, my name is Jesus Mendoza. I'm studying software development and enjoy coding, especially learning Angular. Outside of school, I'm really into fitness and soccer. The gym has been a big part of my life, and I follow teams like Barcelona.I chose software development becuase I've always been curious of how things works behind the scenes and the idea of building something from scratch and watching it come to life intrigues me. I'm looking forward to learning and collaborating with all of you this semester.

My Identity

I'm a future web developer who likes to spend time diving into Angular — particularly Angular reactive forms. I'm working to master modular architecture. I enjoy breaking down complex pieces into more readable parts and using analogies to deepen understanding.

One of my proudest moments was building my own Angular To-Do list using localStorage and Bootstrap.

One of my long-term goals is to build a fitness tracking app that combines my love for health and technology. I want to create something that's accessible and actually helps people stay motivated in their fitness journey.

My Learning Journey

I started getting into web development in high school. It was challenging, but I found it fascinating to create my own page. In this class, I look forward to sharpening my HTML and CSS skills. I also want to explore more modern semantic practices while building web applications. Styling has always been a weakness for me, so I hope this class helps me improve that area I've been neglecting.
